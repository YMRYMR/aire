# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to participate in a respectful, constructive, and technically focused way.

Examples of expected behavior:

- giving actionable technical feedback
- being respectful in disagreement
- assuming good faith
- keeping discussions relevant to the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory or abusive language
- deliberate disruption of community spaces
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate them.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when someone is representing the project.
